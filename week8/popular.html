The most popular heroes in the current set of cards are:
Axe.
Drow Ranger.
Phantom Assassin.
Luna.
Axe is a red hero who has a awesome stat line with a great signature card that allows him to battle 3 enemies at once.
Drow Ranger has a mediocre stat line, but her passive gives every ally 1 extra attack and her signature card silences all enemy heroes preventing anything from being played.
Phantom Assassin has a good stat line, but gains 4 attack when attacking heroes and can cast her signature card to instantly kill an enemy hero.
Luna has a bad stat line, but makes up for it with a passive that deals 1 damage randomly to any enemy. Every time this passive triggers she gets a charge onto her signature card. When the card is played it deals 3 damage randomly to an enemy once per each charge.
